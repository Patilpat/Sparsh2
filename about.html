The story of Parissparsh Jewellers is no fairy tale but an epic scripted meticulously by two generations of the Parissarsh clan. It all started way back in the 21th century, the group gets its name from the late abaso Sadashiv Pavale, a doyen of the family and the chief architect who captured the hearts and imagination of the people with his ethics and his jewellery.


With unbeatable trust gained at chain of stores in Gargoti, Parissparsh Jeweler's took Gargoti to enrich its market-hold. While purity and transparency continue to earn the trust scores of customers, what also sets the brand apart is the wide variety comprising of Gold necklaces, bangles, chains, mangalsutras, ear-rings, rings etc. Through various intricately crafted collections, the brand has distinct styles to offer.


Parissparsh Jewellers embodies the values of trust, honesty and purity. This combination of exceptional quality and deep rooted values hold testimony to the trust held by thousands of satisfied customers.All materials are responsibly sourced and we ensure that the Stones, gold and silver sold at our stores are absolutely pure.


With their philosophy of 'Customer First', the brand is all set to create a timeless, golden bond with its customers. If there's one simple adage that describes the Parissparsh Jewellers Group;it is purity of thoughts and deeds. Since 2017, purity has been the guiding philosophy in every aspect of our business - from the quality of raw materials to finished designs; from business ethics to honest transactions with customers.


- We offer gold in 18k and 22k jewellery, and 14k gold in the form of bullion.
- Our large collection of pre-selected stones is tested for quality in terms of the cut, color, and clarity the stones are then evaluated based on a standardized grading scale.
- To ensure the quality, all the jewellery is displayed with the hallmark.
